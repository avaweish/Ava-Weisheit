Caitlin Clark & WNBA Attendance

Analyzing the influence of Caitlin Clark on professional women's basketball attendance and social media trends.

Overview

This ongoing project explores how Caitlin Clark, a standout college basketball player, influences WNBA attendance. We also analyze Google Trends data to understand how social media recognition may affect fan engagement. By combining sports analytics with digital trends, the project aims to quantify her impact on professional women's basketball viewership.

My Role

I am responsible for data collection, preprocessing, and analysis. This includes scraping HTML pages and APIs for attendance and social media data, creating visually appealing graphs to display trends, and performing linear regression to determine the relationship between Caitlin Clark’s presence and WNBA attendance. I interpret statistical results and continuously update the project as new data becomes available.

Skills Demonstrated

Web Scraping (HTML & APIs) • Data Cleaning & Wrangling • Data Visualization • Linear Regression Analysis • Statistical Interpretation • Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn)
