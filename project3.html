Athlete Shark Tank (Pitch-an-Athlete)

Leadership, branding strategy, and teamwork in a competitive presentation setting.

Overview

This project centered around creating and pitching an athlete brand modeled after the format of the TV show Shark Tank. In today’s sports industry, athletes build their identities both on and off the field — and this project challenged teams to research, develop, and present a comprehensive brand strategy for an assigned athlete to a mock panel of investors.

My Role

I served as the team leader, coordinating all aspects of our presentation and group collaboration. I organized team meetings, delegated responsibilities, and ensured that all deliverables were completed on time and met professional standards. My role also included reviewing and editing materials before submission and facilitating communication between team members.

Key Achievements

  • Led a team of peers through planning, research, and presentation development.
  • Oversaw task delegation, scheduling, and submission logistics.
  • Guided creative direction and narrative structure of our “athlete pitch.”
  • Recognized by our professor and a panel of guest “sharks” for having the best overall presentation.

Skills Demonstrated

Leadership • Team Collaboration • Time Management • Branding Strategy • Public Speaking • Presentation Design

Deliverables

  • Final Presentation Slides
  • Athlete Research Summary
